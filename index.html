I pride myself in being someone that could be relied on, and someone who does not need a lot of hand-holding. I also try to be an uplifting spirit in the workplace, and don't shy away from extra responsibility.

Coding

When I code, I generally try to make the code as simple as it can possibly be, while maintaining necessary scalability. I try to comment everything, so that I can go back and quickly understand what and why I did something. Overall, I am learning not to overthink a project, but rather plan it out, code it, and complete enough testing to make sure my code is working properly.

Because we all work together remotely and we all had different levels of experience with the languages we used, I had to format my work in a way that could be easily scaled within our project and in a way that my teammates could easily understand. I communicated really well with my teammates and took the lead on managing our project to completion. Though it was challenging, I believe we all learned a lot and had fun with it.
